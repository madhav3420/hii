F.A.Q

  • What are the check-in and check-out times?

    Check-in time is Mon - Sun : 12:00 PM - 12:00 PM / 12:00 AM - 12:00 AM. However, these times may vary based on the hotel's policy and availability.

  • Is parking available on-site?

    Yes Parking is available on site.

  • Can I modify or cancel my reservation?

    Reservation modification and cancellation policies vary based on the type of reservation and the hotel's terms. It's recommended to review the specific cancellation policy when making a reservation.

  • How do I request additional amenities?

    You can make requests for extra amenities by calling the front desk or contacting guest services. Many hotels also offer the option to make requests through their mobile apps.

  • Is breakfast included in the room rate?

    Some hotels include breakfast with the room rate, while others may offer it as an add-on or require a separate payment. Check the details of your reservation to find out if breakfast is included.

  • How do I request a wake-up call?

    You can request a wake-up call by contacting the front desk or guest services. Many hotels also provide an option to set a wake-up call through their in-room phone system.
